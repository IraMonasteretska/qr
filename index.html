BrainQR procedures

Brain neurofeedback

Brain Neurofeedback, also known as EEG (Electroencephalogram) Biofeedback, is a cutting-edge therapeutic technique that involves monitoring and training the brain's electrical activity. This technique is a powerful way to address cognitive and emotional symptoms in the context of post concussion syndrome

Learn more

Muscles biofeedback

Muscle biofeedback involves using electromyography (EMG) technology to measure and provide real-time feedback on the electrical activity within muscles. MuscleQR offers individuals comprehensive tools to assist in post-concussion recovery holistically

Learn more

Heart rate variability

HRV is a natural variation between heartbeats that reflects the adaptability and balance in the autonomic nervous systems. Our HRV Biofeedback Program at MuscleQR involves monitoring the variability of heartbeats and providing feedback in real time

Learn more

Breathing belt sensors biofeedback

Breathing Belt sensors biofeedback is an innovative, integrative method designed to improve wellness and resilience for individuals who are navigating the post-concussion healing process. Empower your recovery with the transformative support of MuscleQR
