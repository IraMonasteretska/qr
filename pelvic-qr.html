Did you know there are over 200,000,000 people suffering silently from a condition which affects almost every aspect of daily life? Imagine being unable to laugh with confidence, enjoy intimate moments, or exercise without anxiety. People with pelvic dysfunction face these hidden challenges.25 % of women in the United States suffer from pelvic floor disorders. This is over 40 million women.

Men are also affected by this condition, with 1 in every 6 men over 40 years old experiencing symptoms. What is the total cost per year of treating this condition? A staggering $12 billion. Here's a revelation that will change everything: These conditions aren't just treatable, they can be prevented and reversed with the right approach. We'll look at how the latest biofeedback and Neurofeedback technologies can transform lives and offer hope and practical solutions to millions.

Let's first talk about the impact on women's health

Pelvic Floor Weakness: Common Causes in Women

  • Pregnancy, childbirth and breastfeeding
  • Obesity
  • Hormonal changes during Menopause
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Childbirth
  • High impact physical activity and stress
  • Constipation can be a chronic condition
  • Other gynecologic disorders
  • Previous injuries to the pelvic area

Sexual difficulties reduce vaginal discomfort and sensation during sexual activity.

Recent studies have shown that up to 50 percent of women aged over 50 suffer from some form of pelvic dysfunction. This number increases to 80 percent for women older than 80. According to a 2023 systematic review published in the Journal of Women's Health, 1 in 4 women will experience significant pelvic issues in their lifetime.
